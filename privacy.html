What this is

Sweetsteak is a sweepstake game for friends and colleagues following the World Cup. This policy explains what information the app collects, why, and how it's handled.

What we collect

  • Apple ID identifier — when you sign in with Apple, we store the unique identifier Apple gives us for your account on this app, plus your name if Apple shares it (only provided the first time you authorise the app).
  • Game data — the games you create or join (by their game code), the player name you pick within a game, and whether you're the game's admin.
  • Draw & score data — the teams assigned to each player, and match results used to calculate the leaderboard.

We don't collect payment information. The prize pot is organised directly between players — Sweetsteak doesn't process, hold, or move money.

Why we collect it

  • To run the sweepstake: assigning teams, keeping score, and showing the leaderboard.
  • To send you notifications about your drawn teams (kick-offs, goals, results) — this is the only thing your sign-in is used for.
  • To let you (or a game's admin) manage a game — viewing its code, resetting a draw, etc.

We don't use your data for advertising, and we don't sell or share it with third parties for marketing. There's no analytics or tracking SDK in the app.

Who else sees it

  • Apple — handles the sign-in itself; see Apple's own privacy policy for what Apple retains.
  • football-data.org — the source of live match scores and fixtures shown in the app. We send them no personal data; we only read public match data from them.
  • Vercel / Neon (Postgres) — our hosting and database providers, who store game data on our behalf. They don't use it for their own purposes.

How long we keep it

Game data is kept for as long as the game exists (typically for the duration of the tournament and a short period after, so you can still see final results). Your sign-in identifier is kept on your device and removed if you log out.

Your choices

  • Log out (More tab) signs you out of Apple on that device — your game stays saved so you can sign back in.
  • Leave game removes a specific game from your device entirely.
  • To ask us to delete your data outright, get in touch (below) with your game code and we'll remove it.

Children

Sweetsteak isn't directed at children and we don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes meaningfully, we'll update the date above.
