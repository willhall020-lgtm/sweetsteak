Entry & the draw

  1. £5 each, paid before the draw. No pay, no play.
  2. All 48 teams are ranked by FIFA world ranking and split into Tier A (1–14), Tier B (15–28) and Tier C (29–42). The bottom six form the Wildcard Pot.
  3. Everyone draws one team blind from each tier — one contender, one mid-table maybe, one team already booked on flights home.
  4. The six wildcards go to six names from a hat. A free fourth team. It will almost certainly do nothing.
