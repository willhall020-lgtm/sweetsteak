The small print

Entry & the draw

  1. Entry fee paid before the draw. No pay, no play.
  2. Opening round: the top N teams (by FIFA ranking) are shuffled and assigned blind — one per player. This is the fun bit.
  3. Balancing rounds: remaining teams are assigned automatically in rank order, each going to whichever player has the lowest total value so far. Ties broken at random.
  4. All 48 teams are assigned exactly once. Players with worse opening picks end up with more teams — that's the point.
