Entry & the draw

  1. £5 each, paid before the draw. No pay, no play.
  2. 48 teams split into Tier A (1–14), Tier B (15–28), Tier C (29–42) by FIFA ranking. Bottom six form the Wildcard Pot.
  3. Each player draws one team blind from Tier A, B, and C.
  4. Six wildcards go to six lucky names from a hat — a free fourth team. Manage expectations.
